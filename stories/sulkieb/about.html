I have a cat named Breadstick. I called Breadstick Breadstick because I like breadsticks and I also like Breadstick the cat. Breadstick is grey and stripy and has a stubby tail. Breadstick sleeps a lot.

I came from a village in the East but now I live in a city by the sea. I work as a courier for a company called Timothy’s Fleet Feet Pickup and Delivery. I think Timothy still owns the company but he is a strange man and we haven’t seen him in years so Nene runs the company now.

I have a musical instrument called Rebecca. She has three strings and a bow. Breadstick didn’t like the sound when I first started playing Rebecca but now I’m much better.
