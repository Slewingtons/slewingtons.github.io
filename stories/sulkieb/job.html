Sulkie's Job

I work for a company called Timothy’s Fleet Feet Pickup & Delivery.

Safe Delivery Guarantee

Fleet Feet delivers packages to people in the city and some of the packages are dangerous. That’s why we have the Safe Delivery Guarantee. The Safe Delivery Guarantee means that every package we deliver is guaranteed to be safe, or if it isn’t it has a red sticker on it.

We have a whole department of wizards who check for dangerous magic spells and put red stickers on the dangerous packages.

When Timothy was in charge couriers were considered “expendable” but Nene put a stop to that.

My Role

I am the number one courier in the company and the courier supervisor. I deliver packages and I also assign packages to other couriers.

Employees

Timothy

Timothy is a strange man who might still own the company but we haven’t seen him in years.

When I first joined the company Timothy made us work a seven day “week” even though everyone else uses a tenday.

What a strange man.

Nene

Nene used to work in the sorting department until Timothy left and she became the general manager. She is very good at her job and everyone says so.

We are special friends but when we are at work we are “professional” which means we don’t hold hands unless it’s an emergency.

Nene has black curly hair and she’s a wizard.

Symphony

Symphony is a bureaumancer and the sorting supervisor.

She wears spectacles and ties her hair in a “bun” except when she “lets loose” and “goes wild.” She wants to run her own branch of Fleet Feet someday.

I’ve been told that she is “half of an elf” but I don’t know what that means.

Willoughby

Willoughby is our friendly staff cleric. He heals any of the couriers who get injured on the job.

He always looks tired and has a huge floppy wizard hat and also he never speaks. Nene invented a spell that gives him “subtitles” so we can understand him.

Willoughby is an elf and has pointy ears.

Luza

Luza is the treasurer who guards Fleet Feet’s money.

It’s very difficult to get Luza to pay people’s wages. You have to let her pay herself at the same time or she thinks it’s not fair.

She is a little magic lizard girl.

Elwood

Elwood is our doorman. He was the first ever courier to work at Fleet Feet back when Timothy started it.

Because of an accident Elwood has been trapped inside our front door for years now. Nene is working on a spell to free him.

Breadstick

Breadstick is my cat but Nene said Breadstick can also be the company mascot.
