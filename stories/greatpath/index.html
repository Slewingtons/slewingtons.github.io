The Great Path

The Story

A short story in a fantasy world, featuring Sulkie, an orphaned girl who wanders the world following the Great Path of her imagination.

Where is she from? Even she doesn't know the answer to that question. She has more pressing things on her mind, such as how she's going to pay for bread.

Features

  • Six story branches.
  • A poor girl who needs your help.
